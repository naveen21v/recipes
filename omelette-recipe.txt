Ingredients:
 - 2 Eggs
 - 1/3 cup onion
 - 1 medium finely chopped chili (jalapeno)
 - 1 tsp. finely chopped cilantro
 - 1/8 tsp. salt
 - 1 tsp. vegetable oil
 - 1 pinch of turmeric powder
 - 1 pinch of black pepper powder 

Directions:

 1. Brown the onion in a large wok. 
 2. Beat eggs, turmeric powder, pepper powder and salt in small bowl until blended.
 3. Add browned onion, chili and cilantro to the mixture and beat further.
 4. Heat vegetable oil in 6 to 8-inch nonstick omelet pan or skillet over medium-high heat 
    until hot. Tilt pan to coat bottom. Pour in egg mixture. Mixture should set immediately at edges.
 5. Gently push cooked portions from edges toward the center with inverted turner so that uncooked 
    eggs can reach the hot pan surface. Continue cooking, tilting pan and gently moving cooked portions 
    as needed.
 6. When top surface of eggs is thickened and no visible liquid egg remains, With a quick flip of the 
    wrist, turn pan and Invert or Slide omelette onto plate. Serve immediately.
